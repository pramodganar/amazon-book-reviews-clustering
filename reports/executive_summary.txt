

AMAZON BOOK REVIEWS CLUSTERING PROJECT
=====================================

PROJECT OBJECTIVE:
Cluster customer reviews using NLP and unsupervised learning.

KEY ACHIEVEMENTS:
- Implemented TF-IDF vectorization
- Applied PCA dimensionality reduction
- Compared KMeans, Agglomerative and DBSCAN
- Generated business-level customer segments
- Built customer review clusters

BUSINESS VALUE:
- Customer segmentation
- Review behavior understanding
- Recommendation improvement
- Review pattern discovery

BEST MODEL:
KMeans Clustering
